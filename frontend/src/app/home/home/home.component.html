Dolphin

An Angular Website

Dophins represents friendship and loyalty, both to humans and to their own species, is the dolphin. Dolphins have rescued humans from shark attacks, and if another dolphin is in mortal danger, others will appear to help it keep its blowhole above the surface of the water. Hence, we decided to name our app on the name of the animal Dolphin to offer a friendly and a relaxing envronment for users to share their feelings.

Mental Health

Mental health is immensely important at every stage of life, from childhood and adolescence through adulthood. Journaling helps control your symptoms and improve your mood by: Helping you prioritize problems, fears, and concerns. Tracking any symptoms day-to-day so that you can recognize triggers and learn ways to better control them. Hence, we aim to provide an opportunity for positive self-talk and identifying negative thoughts to combat mental health problems, with the help of this website.

Importance of the website

This is a free to use website, which will track your daily moods, activities and feelings and will generate recommendations based on that. Moreover, it allows you to revisit sad or happy moments from your life with a calender to focus on the improvements the user has made.

Angular 10

Angular 10 is a TypeScript-based open-source web application framework led by the Angular Team at Google and by a community of individuals and corporations.

MongoDB

MongoDB is a source-available cross-platform document-oriented database program. It is classified as a NoSQL database program and uses JSON-like documents with optional schemas.

Bootstrap 5

Bootstrap is a free and open-source CSS framework directed at responsive, mobile-first front-end web development, for typography, forms, buttons, navigation, and other interface components.
